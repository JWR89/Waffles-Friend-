Who Is Jesus?

Jesus is the heart of every Waffles & Friends adventure! He's more than just a name we hear — He's our best friend, our Savior, and the reason we have love, joy, and hope. Jesus isn’t far away or hard to find—He walks with us every day, right here in Mercy Meadows and in your heart too! He helps us when we’re scared, celebrates when we’re happy, and never stops loving us. That’s why every story we tell points back to Him!

The Greatest Story Ever Told

Jesus is God's Son, sent to show the world what love looks like. He came to teach us how to love others, to forgive, to be kind, and to always trust in God. But He didn’t just teach — He gave His life for us, so we could live forever with Him! He healed the hurting, welcomed the lonely, and showed everyone—even kids!—that they were deeply loved. And the best part? He didn’t stay gone. Jesus came back to life, proving that nothing is too big for God's love!

Jesus Loves You

No matter who you are or what you’ve done, Jesus loves you with all His heart. He knows your name, He cares about your dreams, and He wants to be your forever friend. You don’t have to be perfect—Jesus loves you just the way you are. He wants to walk with you, laugh with you, and help you become everything God made you to be!

How Can I Know Jesus?

You can talk to Jesus anytime — just like a friend! He’s always listening, even when you whisper or don’t know what to say. You can also read about Him in the Bible, sing songs to Him, draw pictures, or just sit quietly and feel His love. We made this site to help you get closer to Him every day—because the more you know Jesus, the more you’ll see how much He treasures you!
